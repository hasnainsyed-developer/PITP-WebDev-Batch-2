Instructions & Pledges

Student Pledge
  1. I will be regular and punctual.
  2. I will devote all my energies to the achievement of better academic results.
  3. I will appear in all tests and examinations conducted by the school/college from time to time during the academic session.
  4. I will faithfully abide by the rules of the school / college discipline and under all circumstances remain loyal and honest to my Parents, Teachers.
  5. I will never indulge myself in any kind of unhealthy activities and will always strive to uphold the dignity and traditions of my institution.
Parent/Guardian Pledge
  1. I under take responsibility of sending my Son/Daughter/ Ward regularly in time to school / college.
  2. In Case, the child is unable to attend school / college, on any day, I will request the Principal / Class Teacher in writing to grant him/her leave.
  3. I will pay the school / college Fees on or before 10th of every month, regularly.
  4. I will provide child with the prescribed uniform and see that the child attains classes everyday in clean uniform.
  5. I will cooperate with the authorities and do my best to carry out the suggestions given from time to time regarding the progress of my child.
  6. I do accept the TC given to my child after three consecutive warnings / notices.
  7. I fully agree and abide by the note given above.
